About Us

Galaxy Boys Hostel has a rich legacy of academic excellence and holistic development. Our mission is to nurture young minds through a student-centered approach.

Galaxy Boys Hostel is a dedicated boys-only hostel located at Ghari Awang Leikai, Imphal West, Manipur. Managed by Sir Surjit, a respected teacher from Comet School, Imphal, the hostel provides a safe, comfortable, and disciplined environment for students. With a strong focus on academics and personal development, Galaxy Boys Hostel also encourages physical activity and recreation. The premises include a football playground, basketball court, and table tennis area, promoting a healthy and active lifestyle. All rooms are equipped with CCTV surveillance to ensure the safety and security of every resident.
